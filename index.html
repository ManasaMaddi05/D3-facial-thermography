🩺 Facial Temperature Tracker

Quickly spot potential fevers or facial heat anomalies using ambient-aware thermal mapping, empowering smarter, faster wellness checks at a glance.

🧠 Purpose: Monitor facial temperature zones in real time using advanced thermographic data. Users can adjust thresholds, detect fevers, and visualize key thermal indicators to assist medical analysis.

💡 Temperature Color Legend

How to Use

  1. Select your current ambient-temperature range from the dropdown.
  2. Click on a marked faical circles to begin adjusting its temperature.
  3. Slide the thermometer bar to simulate your measured temperature.
  4. Toggle “Show Medical Alerts” to visualize regions ≥ 38 °C in red, which may indicate a potential fever. These areas will glow red to signal elevated heat that could require further attention.
  5. Filter data by age and gender to explore subgroup patterns.
  6. Hover on a region to display its temperature with a tooltip.
  7. Use the color legend to interpret readings as low, normal, or high.
  8. Check the Auto Diagnosis panel to see a summary of any regions showing elevated temperatures. It provides potential interpretations (e.g., fever, sinus issues) along with suggested actions like rest, hydration, or consulting a healthcare provider
  9. Disclaimer: This dashboard is for informational use only—not a replacement for medical advice or diagnosis.
