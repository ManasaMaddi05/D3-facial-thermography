🩺 Smart Thermal Facial Dashboard

🧠 Purpose: Monitor facial temperature zones in real time using advanced thermographic data. Users can adjust thresholds, detect fevers, and visualize key thermal indicators to assist medical analysis.

How to Use

  • Select your current ambient‑temperature range.
  • Click on a facial region to select it.
  • Use the slider to enter your measured temperature.
  • Check “Show Medical Alerts” to highlight fever zones (≥ 38 °C).
  • Filter by age & gender for subgroup differences.
  • Hover over a region for exact values in a tooltip.
  • Consult the legend below for low/normal/high color codes.

How to Use

  • Select your current ambient‐temperature range.
  • Click on a facial region in the diagram to select it.
  • Use the slider to enter your measured temperature for the selected area.
  • Check “Show Medical Alerts” to highlight any regions ≥ 38 °C with a red glow.
  • Filter by age and gender to view subgroup differences.
  • Hover over a region to see the exact temperature in a tooltip.
  • Consult the legend at the bottom to interpret low, normal, and high readings.
  • Disclaimer: For informational purposes only—this is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment.

Purpose

  • Provide a real‑time, interactive view of facial temperature zones.
  • Allow users to simulate and compare readings between ambient coditions, age, and gender
  • Highlight potential fever regions (≥ 38 °C) for preliminary screening.
  • Serve as a quick‐reference tool for clinicians, researchers, and educators.
